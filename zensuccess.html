02 · Why ZenSuccess

Customer Success is a Measurement Layer, Not Just a Team.

The biggest mistake in B2B CS is treating success as a post-sale function. ZenSuccess embeds CS intelligence at every stage — from the first sales touch to lifetime advocacy.

📏

Measured at every stage, not just at renewal

CS success is not a single score at contract renewal. It is a measurement taken at every stage of the journey — from onboarding through adoption, health monitoring, and expansion. ZenSuccess makes the continuous measurement visible and actionable.

🎯

Proactive intervention, not reactive firefighting

By the time a customer flags a problem, you are already behind. ZenSuccess detects churn signals 30–60 days before they become visible — giving your CSMs time to intervene with context, not just effort.

🔀

Two distinct CS models for two types of engagement

Product/SaaS and Consulting/Services require fundamentally different CS measurement frameworks. One size does not fit all. ZenSuccess deploys the right model for your business — with the right signals for each engagement type.

04 · CS Models

Two Engagement Types. Two Distinct CS Models.

Product/SaaS and Consulting/Services engagements have fundamentally different success drivers. ZenSuccess applies the right CS measurement framework for each — automatically.

🖥️
Product / SaaS CS Model
What ZenSuccess monitors for software and platform engagements
🚀
Onboarding Success
Day 1–30
›
Time-to-first-value (TTV) — Did the customer achieve their first measurable outcome?
›
Seat activation rate — % of purchased licences activated within 30 days
›
Integration health — APIs connected and data flowing correctly
📈
Product Adoption
Day 30–90
›
DAU/MAU ratio — Daily active users vs monthly active users (habit indicator)
›
Feature adoption breadth — Which features are being used; which are being missed
›
Power user growth — Are champions forming inside the account?
⚠️
Churn Risk Signals
Ongoing
›
Login frequency drop >25% in any 30-day window → immediate flag
›
Champion departure — Key contact changes role or leaves the company
›
Support escalation pattern — Increasing severity or frequency of tickets
🔒
Renewal Intelligence
90-day runway
›
Composite health score — Updated daily across all five health factors
›
ROI evidence — Measurable value documented and ready for renewal conversation
›
Expansion readiness — New use case signals, seat growth indicators, upsell timing

Consulting / Services CS Model
What ZenSuccess monitors for consulting and professional services engagements
🏁
Project Kickoff

›
Scope completeness — Agreed outputs, timelines, and dependencies clearly defined

Executive sponsor engagement — Is the sponsor accessible and actively involved?

›

Risk Signals

Scope creep pattern — Accumulating change requests undermining original value

05 · Account Intelligence

Account Management Intelligence Stack.

Post-customer B2B intelligence — four layers of signal capture that build the complete picture of every account, continuously updated in real time.

⚙️Layer 1 · Technographic & Product Signals
🖥️ Product / SaaS
›Login frequency & DAU/MAU trends
›Feature adoption depth by user cohort
›API usage depth and integration health
🤝 Consulting / Services
›Milestone completion rate weekly
›Deliverable acceptance vs revision cycles
›Budget consumption vs delivery ratio
🤝Layer 2 · Relationship & Stakeholder Intelligence
🖥️ SaaS
›Champion stability (tenure & role)
›Executive sponsor engagement frequency
›Multi-thread depth across account
🤝 Services
›Sponsor accessibility & alignment
›Satisfaction by level (C-suite to Manager)
›Knowledge transfer completion quality

🖥️ SaaS Risk Flags
›Usage decline >20% in 30 days → flag
›Champion departure via LinkedIn detected
›Competitive tool adoption → displacement risk
🤝 Services Risk Flags
›Milestone slippage >10% → delivery risk
›Sponsor change → continuity risk
›New client initiative → expansion signal

🖥️ SaaS
›90-day runway: health score + ROI evidence
›Expansion readiness: seat growth, new use cases
›Multi-year viability based on satisfaction signals
🤝 Services
›Next phase SOW pipeline + close probability
›New strategic problem → follow-on opportunity
›Reference case study readiness assessment
