Leadership

Rahul Garapaty

Chief Executive Officer

Rahul is a seasoned executive with over two decades of experience spanning engineering, product management, and sales leadership. His career includes roles at Texas Instruments, Motorola, Persistent Systems, and Tech Mahindra, and he is co-founder of Socxo. He has led go-to-market strategy for solutions spanning AI/ML, telecom, IoT, digital, cloud, and martech. Today, his focus is on helping organizations translate emerging AI capabilities into practical, high-impact outcomes. He holds an MBA from the Kellogg School of Management, an MS in Computer Science from UT Dallas, and a BS in Electrical Engineering from the National Institute of Technology, Jamshedpur.

Sudarsan Rao

Chief Product Officer

Sudarsan is a seasoned entrepreneur, technologist, and venture advisor with over two decades of experience building and scaling global product startups. His career spans enterprise SaaS, middleware, digital transformation, AI, and martech — across software, marketing, fintech, healthtech, and 3D printing. At Vaakya Technologies, he led the development of a sovereign middleware platform, earning four US patents. He is also founder of Socxo, and at Zenspring, he guides founders and enterprises through AI transformation and funding strategy. His expertise spans strategic consulting, business strategy, product marketing, IT consulting, and leadership development across US, Europe, Singapore and India.
